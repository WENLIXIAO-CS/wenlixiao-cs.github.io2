About me

I am senior majoring Electrical Information Engineering (Computer Engineering stream) at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen.

I have interned at Robotics Institute Summer Scholar (RISS) at Carneign Mellon University, where I collaborated with Prof. John Dolan and Yiwei Lyu in safe Multi-agent Reinforcement Learning.

When visiting Berkeley, I had the hornor to work with Tianjun Zhang. We investigated interesting topics about the application of contrastive larning on Goal-conditioned Reinforcement Learning.

Previously, I had the pleasure to be a research assistant in the NCEL Lab at CUHK, Shenzhen, collarborating with Prof. Jianwei Huang and Prof. Bing Luo. I have been interned at Shenzhen Institute of Artificial Intelligence and Robotics for Society (AIRS).

My current research interests lie in the intersection field of Autonomous Systems, Reinforcement Learning and Robotics.
